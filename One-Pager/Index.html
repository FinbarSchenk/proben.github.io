Resident Evil 2 is a classic survival horror game developed by Capcom. Code:_Veronica
Claire Redfield searches for her brother Chris after being abducted by the Umbrella Corporation. She ends up on Rockford Island, a research facility hit by a T-Virus outbreak. Teaming up with fellow prisoner Steve Burnside, they plan their escape. Meanwhile, Albert Wesker, a former S.T.A.R.S. leader and double agent, seeks a sample of the T-Veronica Virus created by Alexia Ashford. A twist reveals Alexia is not on the island but in cryogenic sleep in another Umbrella facility in Antarctica after injecting herself with the T-Veronica Virus.

Resident_Evil 5 Remake
Resident Evil 5 is a third-person shooter game with cooperative gameplay between Chris and Sheva. It features various enemies, including Majini and Reapers, and environments like urban areas, caves, and ancient ruins. The game offers a variety of weapons, a merchant for purchases, and multiplayer modes for co-op and competitive play. Critics praised the game's cooperative gameplay, graphics, and enemy design, but criticized its linear level design and lack of innovation.
The game, Resident Evil 5, follows Chris Redfield, a member of the BSAA, as he is sent to Kijuju to apprehend arms dealer Ricardo Irving, who deals in Bio-Organic Weapons (BOWs) and associated viruses. In Kijuju, Chris teams up with Sheva Alomar, an African BSAA agent whose parents died in an Umbrella Corporation laboratory incident. The game also reveals a subplot where Chris and his partner Jill Valentine confronted Albert Wesker at the Umbrella founder’s estate, with Wesker overpowering them and Jill seemingly perishing. However, Chris later sees an image of Jill on a PDA, leading him to investigate her fate. Wesker’s goal is to wipe out most of the world’s population using a new supervirus called “Uroboros” and create a genetically superior race of “superhumans” by sparing only those resistant to the virus. He collaborates with Excella Gionne, the head of TriCell, a pharmaceutical company competing with Umbrella, to achieve this.

Resident_Evil 6 Remake
Resident Evil 6 features a mix of action, horror, and exploration elements. The game introduces a new control system, which allows for more fluid movement and combat. Players can also use melee attacks and execute enemies with a new "finishing move" system.
The story follows Jake Muller, son of Albert Wesker, as he flees from authorities during a bio-terrorist attack. He partners with Sherry Birkin to create a vaccine for the C-virus. Meanwhile, Chris Redfield and Piers Nivans lead a BSAA team to combat the infected populace. The story unfolds with multiple plot twists, including the reveal of an imposter Ada Wong and the true identity of Jake Muller. The characters work together to stop the outbreaks and uncover the truth behind the bio-terrorist attacks.
Resident_Evil 5 Remake_&_Resident_Evil 6 Remake
Resident Evil 5 Remake and Resident Evil 6 Remake are two games being developed by Capcom, focusing on multiplayer or coop horror elements. The original titles had coop aspects that differed from previous titles, and the community had mixed opinions. However, the remakes have generally received good reviews, although the community is not as large as the original titles. The trend of previous remakes indicates that the community likes them, and the coop aspect might follow the same trend, potentially leading to higher review scores. The problem with the original titles was their shift from survival horror to action survival horror, resulting in linear shooter gameplay with coop elements. Old RE veterans disliked this change due to its linear and shooter-like gameplay. The hope for old veterans and new players lies in Capcom using the remake option to address the "flaws" mentioned by veterans and incorporate new perspectives. The lower creepiness factor remains an issue, but Capcom could address this by making enemy models scarier and giving them a "creep under your skin" aspect.
